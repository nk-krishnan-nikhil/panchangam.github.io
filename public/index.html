Daily Panchangam feed you can subscribe to in Google Calendar.

Download .ics Open in Calendar (webcal)

Public URL:

https://REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_DOMAIN/malayalam-panchangam.ics

Add to Google Calendar:

  1. Open Google Calendar.
  2. Other calendars -> From URL.
  3. Paste your public .ics URL.
  4. Click Add calendar.

Tip: Replace REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_DOMAIN after enabling GitHub Pages.
